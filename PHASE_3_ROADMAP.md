## 1. Phase Objective
To transition `AccountingFlow` from a **Simulation Engine** to an **Operational Compass**.
We import real-world financial data not just to record history, but to **compare it against the Strategic Baseline** established in Phase 2.

## 2. The Golden Rule of Integration
> **"Real Data enters as COMPARISON, not REPLACEMENT."**

For the first month (Month 1), the system operates in **Read-Only Mode**.
*   ❌ No Automatic Risk Judgments (yet).
*   ❌ No AI Interventions/Alerts (yet).
*   ⭕ **Compare Only:** "Actual vs. Simulation Baseline".

## 3. Implementation Roadmap (The "Safe Route")

### Step 1: Read-Only Integration (Month 1)
*   **Action:** Upload actual accounting slips (Excel/API).
*   **System Output:** "Silent Comparison".
    *   Does the Revenue Structure match the 'Hybrid Acceleration' model?
    *   Is the 'Runway Defense' logic (Grant netting) being applied correctly in real life?

### Step 2: Deviation Cards (The "Delta" View)
Instead of raw numbers, the dashboard will highlight **Deviations**:
*   *Sales Concentration:* "Top 1 Client is 45% (Strategy Gap: +15%)"
*   *AWS Efficiency:* "Infra Cost is 12% higher than Baseline."
*   *Cash Burn:* "Burn Rate is 5% lower than Baseline (Good)."

### Step 3: Verified Intervention (Month 2+)
*   **Condition:** Only unlocked after Month 1 comparison proves stable.
*   **Features:**
    *   AI suggests journal entries (Auto-Journalizing).
    *   Risk Dashboard actively flags "Strategic Violations".
    *   CEO receives "Strategy Correction" alerts.
